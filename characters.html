Main Characters

Ted

A magically alive, bold, and rebellious teddy bear who refuses to play by the rules. Despite his unpredictable, often inappropriate behavior, Ted possesses a surprisingly strong loyalty toward John and always has his back when things get chaotic.

John Bennett

An ordinary sixteen-year-old teenager navigating the terrifying waters of high school. Constantly dealing with school pressures, quirky family dynamics, and the unique, wildly overwhelming experience of having a talking stuffed animal as a best friend.

Matty Bennett

John's father, a gruff, strict, and highly opinionated patriarch of the Bennett household. A proud Bostonian who frequently struggles with Ted's disruptive presence, trying fruitlessly to instill discipline in a highly unconventional home environment.

Susan Bennett

John's relentlessly supportive, sweet-natured mother. She constantly strives to maintain harmony and a sense of balance in their increasingly crazy household, finding ways to see the good in Ted even when he tests her considerable patience.

Blair

John's cousin and a college student living with the Bennett family. She holds deeply progressive views that constantly clash with Matty's traditional, old-school Boston mentality, providing an intellectual counterbalance to the household's chaotic energy.
