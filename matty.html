Scott Grimes

Plays Matty Bennett

Scott Grimes gives a hilarious powerhouse performance as Matty. A long-time collaborator of Seth MacFarlane on 'American Dad' and 'The Orville', Grimes channels aggressive East Coast dad energy masterfully. His rapid-fire delivery and ability to make Matty deeply flawed yet somewhat lovable is a pillar of the show's success.
