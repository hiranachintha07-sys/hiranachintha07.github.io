Seth MacFarlane

Voice of Ted / Creator

Seth MacFarlane is the creator of the series and the original Ted movies. He returns to provide the iconic voice and motion capture performance for Ted. Known for his work on Family Guy and The Orville, MacFarlane infuses Ted with his signature rapid-fire comedic timing and irreverent humor.
