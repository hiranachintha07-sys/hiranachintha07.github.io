Seasons and Episodes

Season 1 7 Episodes

1

Just Say Yes

▼
John struggles with school dynamics and asks Ted for terrible advice to survive the realities of high school life.
2

My Two Dads

▼
Ted decides to step in as a surrogate father figure for John, much to Matty's immense frustration.
3

Ejectile Dysfunction

▼
A hilarious misadventure involving an old VHS tape and a desperate attempt to cover up a mistake before Susan finds out.
4

Subways, Bicycles and Automobiles

▼
Ted and John attempt to navigate Boston's public transit system with disastrous and comedic results.
5

Desperately Seeking Susan

▼
Susan desperately tries to inject culture into the household, but Ted completely derails her plans.
6

Loud Night

▼

7

He's Gotta Have It

▼
John develops a crush, and Ted's wingman tactics prove to be incredibly problematic.

Season 2 8 Episodes

1

Talk Dirty to Me

▼
John and Ted use a phone in the school basement to call a phone‑sex hotline, which ends up costing the school thousands of dollars and brings in a strict school investigator to find out who did it.
2

Mrs. Robicheck

▼
Ted begins a risky affair with a married woman and gets emotionally involved, while Matty and Susan try to figure out what’s causing Matty’s strange health issues.
3

Dungeons & Dealers

▼
A shortage of weed leads John, Ted, and Blaire into a drug dealer’s Dungeons & Dragons‑like adventure in hopes of earning product, with Matty and Susan joining unaware of the real prize.
4

The Mom’s Bombed Rom‑Com

▼
Blaire’s rom‑com marathon frustrates Matty at home, and Ted lies to John about his romantic exploits, leading to an unusual double date with their elderly neighbor.
5

The Sword in the Stoned

▼
Worried about college applications, John and Ted audition for the school play, and meanwhile Matty and Susan take on part‑time jobs at Dunkin’ Donuts.
6

Roe v. Weed

▼
After a casual hookup, Blaire unexpectedly finds herself dealing with a pregnancy situation, causing tension and clashing opinions within the family.
7

Susan Is the New Black

▼
A police officer discovers John and Ted’s weed during a traffic stop; to protect them, Susan takes the blame and experiences what life is like from the inside of jail.
8

Fraudcast News

▼
After a health scare forces Matty to stay calm, the family tries to keep him uninformed about a major media event (the O.J. Simpson verdict), going to extreme lengths to protect him.
